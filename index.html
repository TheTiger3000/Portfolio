About Me

Highly motivated Robotics Student, passionate about technology and automation. Ready to learn something new and improve the skills step by step every day.

Education

Fourth year student at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca in the robotics engineering field. In this years I have studied about how to design machines, robot parts, factories, PLC automation, simulations and programming.

Usual Days

I love to use my imagination and solve different problems. I have learnt to build a habit everyday and stick to it. Beside school hours, I like to read, watch tutorials and practice, play the guitar and sometimes watch a movie or take a walk. For the most part I am a self learner.
